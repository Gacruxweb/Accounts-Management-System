# Accounts-Management-System
A professional financial management and accounting platform designed to streamline business operations, track sales and purchases, manage cash flow, and generate real-time financial reports and insights.

## [Explore Accounts Management System](https://super-shop-ams.vercel.app/)
This application is a comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Accounting Solution designed to streamline business operations. It serves as a central hub for managing financial health, commercial transactions, and organizational transparency. By integrating sales, purchases, banking, and double-entry bookkeeping, it empowers business owners and accountants to maintain precise financial control and generate real-time analytical reports.

## Detailed Key Features
### 1. Financial Intelligence Dashboard
Real-time Metrics: Instant visibility into total sales, net profit margins, and cash flow.
Activity Monitoring: A chronological feed of recent transactions and financial movements.
Performance Trends: Visual charts showing revenue versus expenses to identify growth patterns.

### 2. Sales & Revenue Management
Invoice Lifecycle: Create, track, and manage sales invoices from issuance to payment.
Sales Returns: Process customer returns with automated adjustment of receivables and stock.
Customer Tracking: Maintain a history of transactions and balances for individual customers.
Status Management: Monitor "Paid," "Unpaid," and "Partial" payment statuses at a glance.

### 3. Purchase & Procurement
Bill Management: Record and track purchase bills from suppliers.
Purchase Returns: Manage returns to vendors for damaged or incorrect goods.
Supplier Relations: Track outstanding payables and procurement history with various vendors.
Document Detail: Comprehensive view of bill items including tax, discounts, and "Received Quantity" tracking.

### 4. Banking & Liquidity Control
Multi-Account Tracking: Manage multiple bank accounts, cash boxes, and payment gateways.
Fund Transfers: Securely record internal transfers between different accounts (e.g., Bank to Petty Cash).
Account Profiles: Detailed bank information including account numbers, branch details, and SWIFT codes.

### 5. Multi-Type Voucher System
A versatile system to record every type of financial movement:
Receipts & Payments: Record cash/bank inflows and outflows.
Expense Tracking: Quick entry for utilities, rent, and operational costs.
Salary Disbursement: Manage employee payroll and compensation records.
Contra Vouchers: Specialized entry for internal bank/cash movements.
Advanced Payments: Track prepayments and security deposits.

### 6. Advanced General Ledger
Double-Entry Journal: Professional journal voucher system ensuring balanced debit and credit entries.
Chart of Accounts (CoA): A hierarchical structure of Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Income, and Expenses.
Hierarchy Management: Add custom sub-accounts to any parent category for granular financial reporting.

### 7. Comprehensive Financial Reporting
Profit & Loss (P&L): Detailed income statement showing gross and net profits.
Balance Sheet: Snapshots of the company's financial position (Assets vs. Equities).
Cash Flow Statement: Track where cash is coming from and where it’s going (Operating, Investing, Financing).
Trial Balance: Ensure the mathematical accuracy of the ledger.
Account Statements: Generate detailed transaction ledgers for any specific account.

### 8. Operational & Inventory Tools
Stock Adjustments: Manually update inventory levels for breakage, audits, or discrepancies.
Stock Transfers: Record the movement of products between different warehouses or branches.
Audit Logs: High-level visibility into transaction histories for transparency and accountability.
